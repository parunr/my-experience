Welcome — what I plan to write about here

This site is a working archive of what I’ve built and what I’m learning. Sixteen-plus years of enterprise delivery — SAP, cloud, and now agentic AI — have taught me two things worth writing about:

  1. The failure mode is almost always three layers below where it surfaces. Most of what I’ve learned that’s actually useful came from staying on a problem past the point where “it should work” felt like an answer.
  2. Repeatable patterns beat one-off wins. A reference architecture that ten teams can use is worth more than a heroic delivery no one can repeat.

Expect posts on:

  • Diagnosing silent failures in Copilot Studio, Azure AI Search indexers, Power Automate flows, and the seams between them.
  • Multi-agent architecture patterns — orchestration, state passing, guardrails, and what I’ve stopped trying to make work.
  • Delivery leadership at the intersection of technical depth and commercial narrative.
  • Notes from the field, unfiltered.

Thanks for stopping by.
